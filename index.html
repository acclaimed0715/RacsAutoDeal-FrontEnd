Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a test drive?

You can book a test drive by clicking the "Inquire" button on any vehicle page or calling us directly at 0917 110 6548. Our team will coordinate with you for a schedule.

Do you offer car financing?

Yes! We partner with various banks to provide competitive financing options tailored to your budget. Visit our showroom for a quick computation.

What documents are needed to sell my car?

To sell your car, you'll need the Original OR/CR, valid IDs, and a duly signed Deed of Sale. We can handle the appraisal and offer a fair market price.

Are the vehicles in your inventory inspected?

Absolutely. Every vehicle undergoes a thorough multi-point inspection and detailed reconditioning before being listed to ensure your peace of mind.
